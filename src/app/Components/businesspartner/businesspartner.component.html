    Annexure B

    Code of Conduct

    This code of conduct (“Code”) shall apply to BUSINESS ASSOCIATE (for the purpose of this Code, the term BUSINESS ASSOCIATE shall include BUSINESS ASSOCIATEs and their officers, employees, agents) involved in marketing and distribution of the Products. The BUSINESS ASSOCIATE shall abide by this Code and any BUSINESS ASSOCIATE found to be violating this Code may be blacklisted by FINSPACE, in addition to any other action as may be provided herein.

    1. Tele-calling a Customer

      2. A Customer is to be contacted for sourcing FINSPACE’s products only under the following circumstances:

      1. When Customer has expressed a desire to acquire a Products through internet site/call centre/Branch or has been referred to by another Customer/customer who has given consent for accepting calls on other products of FINSPACE.
      2. When the Customer’s name/telephone number/ address is available and has been taken from one of the lists/directories/databases approved by the BUSINESS ASSOCIATE Manager/Team leader, after taking his/ her consent.
      3. The BUSINESS ASSOCIATE should not call a person whose name/number is flagged in any “do not disturb” list.

    2. When you may contact a Customer on telephone?

      3. Telephonic contact must normally be limited between 09:30 hours and 17:30 hours. However, it may be ensured that a Customer is contacted only when the call is not expected to inconvenience him/her. Calls earlier or later than the prescribed time may be placed when the Customer has expressly authorized BUSINESS ASSOCIATE to do so either in writing or orally.

    3. Can the Customer’s interest be discussed with anybody else?

      4. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE should respect a Customer’s privacy. The Customer’s interest may normally be discussed only with the Customer and any other individual/family member such as Customer’s accountant/secretary /spouse, authorized by the Customer.

    4. Leaving messages and contacting persons other than the Customer.

      5. Calls must first be placed to the Customer. In the event the Customer is not available, a message may be left for him/her. The aim of the message should be to get the Customer to return the call or to check for a convenient time to call again. Ordinarily, such messages may be restricted to:

      “Please leave a message that ______________ (Name of officer) representing FINSPACE called and requested to call back at __________ (phone number)”.
      As a general rule, the message must indicate that the purpose of the call is regarding selling or distributing a products of FINSPACE.

    5. No misleading statements/misrepresentations permitted

      6. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE should not:

      1. Mislead the Customer on any Products offered;
      2. Mislead the Customer about their business or organization's name, or falsely represent themselves;
      3. Make any false / unauthorised commitment on behalf of FINSPACE for any facility/service;
      4. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE shall not assure any favourable consideration by FINSPACE to the Customer;
      5. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE shall not use any inducement, threat, coercion, misrepresentation, unethical or unlawful means and false, incorrect or misleading representations to procure the referral of the potential Customers for availing the loans/ products and adhere to the Code of Conduct issued by FINSPACE in this regard at all times

    6. Telemarketing Etiquettes

      7. PRE-CALL
      1. No calls prior to 09:30 hours or post 17:30 Hours unless specifically requested: No serial dialing
      2. No calling on lists unless list is cleared by team leader
      DURING CALL
      1. Identify yourself, your company and FINSPACE
      2. Request permission to proceed
      3. If denied permission, apologize and politely disconnect.
      4. State reason for your call
      5. Always offer to call back on landline, if call is made to a cell number - Never interrupt or argue
      6. To the extent possible, talk in the language which is most comfortable to the Customer keep the conversation limited to business matters
      7. Check for understanding of “Most Important Terms and Conditions” by the customer, if he plans to buy the products
      8. Reconfirm next call or next visit details
      9. Provide BUSINESS ASSOCIATE’s telephone number name or contact details if asked for by the Customer.
      10. Thank the customer for his/her time
      POST CALL
      1. Customers who have expressed their lack of interest for the offering should not be called for the next 3 months with the same offer
      2. Provide feedback to FINSPACE on Customers who have expressed their desire to be flagged “Do Not Disturb”
      3. Never call or entertain calls from Customers regarding products already sold. Advise them to Contact the Customer Service of FINSPACE.

    7. Gifts or bribes

      8. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE must not accept gifts from Customers or bribes of any kind. Any BUSINESS ASSOCIATE offered a bribe or payment of any kind by a Customer must report the offer to his/her management.

    8. Precautions to be taken on visits/ contacts

      9. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE should:
      1. Respect personal space - maintain adequate distance from the Customer.
      2. Not enter the Customer's residence/office against his/her wishes
      3. Not visit in large numbers - i.e. not more than one BUSINESS ASSOCIATE and one supervisor, if required. Respect the Customer's privacy.
      4. If the Customer is not present and only family members/office persons are present at the time of the visit, he/she should end the visit with a request for the Customer to call back.
      5. Provide his/her telephone number, name or the contact details, if asked for by the Customer.
      6. Limit discussions with the Customer to the business - Maintain a professional distance.

    9. Other important aspects - Appearance & Dress Code

      10. BUSINESS ASSOCIATE's must be appropriately dressed -

      For men this means
      1. Well ironed trousers;
      2. Well ironed shirt, shirt sleeves preferably buttoned down.
      For women this means
      1. Well ironed formal attire (Saree, Suit etc.)
      2. Well-groomed appearance (Jeans and/or T Shirt, open sandals are not considered appropriate under formal attire)

    10. Handling of letters & other communication

      11. Any communication sent to the Customer should be only in the mode and format approved by FINSPACE.
